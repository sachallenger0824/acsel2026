Submit Your Paper for ACSEL 2026!

ACSEL 2026, with the theme ‘AI in Accident Prevention’, focuses on how artificial intelligence can enhance accident prevention, autonomous risk management, and the transformation and innovation of laboratory safety education.

Submit Abstract for Oral or Poster Presentation!

Abstract Submission Guidelines

Submissions are encouraged in (but not limited to) the following areas:

  • AI-Based Risk Prediction
  • Intelligent Laboratory Safety Monitoring
  • AI Early Warning Systems
  • AI for Safety Education
  • Human Behavior Analysis in Accident Prevention
  • AI and Smart Laboratory Systems
  • AI-Based Accident Analysis
  • AI for Emergency Response
  • Laboratory Safety and Health Management
  • Laboratory Safety and Health Compliance
  • Laboratory Waste Treatment and Management
  • Other Laboratory Safety and Health Related Issues

Abstract should be no more than 100 words. Each author with an accepted abstract will be invited to give an oral or poster presentation.

(Based on these submissions we will make a decision as whether or not to accept your contribution and arrange the conference sessions.)
