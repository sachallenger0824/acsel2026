Getting to Campus

如何抵達中國文化大學

directions_bus

By Bus / 搭公車

From MRT Jiantan Station (劍潭捷運站) or Shilin Station (士林站)

  • Bus 260 ~30 min
  • Minibus 小18 ~25 min
  • Minibus 小19 ~25 min

Board at Jiantan MRT Station (劍潭站) and alight at 文化大學 stop.

Buses run frequently; real-time arrival info available via the 台北等公車 app.

local_taxi

Self-Driving & Taxi / 自駕 & 計程車

Chinese Culture University, 55 Hwa-Kang Rd., Shilin District, Taipei 111
台北市士林區華岡路55號

  • From Taipei City Centre ~30–45 min
  • Taxi fare (approx.) 500–700 TWD

Navigate to 中國文化大學 (Chinese Culture University) via Yangmingshan National Park road. Parking is available on campus.

Taxi apps: Line Taxi or 55688 (traditional taxi call).

flight_land

From TPE Airport / 桃園機場出發

Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (桃園國際機場) → Chinese Culture University

  • Airport MRT + Bus ~90 min
  • Taxi / Private Transfer ~60–90 min
  • Taxi fare (approx.) 1,200–1,600 TWD

By MRT + Bus: Take the Airport MRT to Taipei Main Station (台北車站) → transfer to MRT Red Line to Jiantan Station (劍潭站) → take Bus 260 / 小18 / 小19 to 文化大學 stop.

By Taxi: Metered taxis are available outside both terminals 24/7. Show the driver this address: 台北市士林區華岡路55號.
